About Pawsy

Connecting loving homes with pets in need.

Our Mission

Pawsy was created with a simple goal: to make pet adoption easier, more transparent, and more human.

Every day, thousands of animals wait in shelters for a chance at a better life. At the same time, people are looking for companionship, connection, and purpose.

Pawsy bridges that gap — helping shelters showcase their pets and making it effortless for adopters to find their perfect companion.

What We Believe In

Care

Every pet deserves a safe, loving home and a second chance.

Connection

Bringing people and pets together in meaningful ways.

Trust

Supporting shelters with tools that make adoption transparent and reliable.
